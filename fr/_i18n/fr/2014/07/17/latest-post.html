Together We Can!

Nederlands • English

July 21st, 2014

Yesterday my sister-in-law and I paid a visit to Dr Saskia Lesnik-Oberstein at LUMC in Leiden, The Netherlands. Dr Lesnik-Oberstein introduced us to her PhD student, Julie Rutten, who had prepared a presentation about the status of CADASIL research at LUMC. It would be impossible for me to convey here the depth and the ingenuity of their research. Let me just say that the more I listened, the more I became convinced that if anyone can find a cure for CADASIL, it is Dr Lesnik-Oberstein and her team. That is why, today, I am setting up this website and am writing this article. Because there was something else that became increasingly clear to me as I listened: however brilliant and however dedicated these researchers are, they cannot arrive at their goal alone. They need the support of a strong patient advocacy group in order to clear a number of practical hurdles for them. So if you have ever hesitated to come forward, hesitate no longer. The time is now. The goal is in sight but the obstacles loom ever stronger. Join our family in setting up an advocacy group, write me at tom@cadasil.eu.
